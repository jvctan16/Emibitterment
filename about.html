About

What is Embitterment?

Embitterment is a social networking site that focuses on information sharing between people. Information about those that are in need. People that are in need. Embitterment's goal is to provide a network hub that allows anyone to share information no matter how insignificant to others whether it a street child begging for alms, or even a group of out of school youth that wishes to go to school. Information that will show everyone that has helped, that needs help, even those that some may think that are beyond help.

What's behind Embitterment?

Embitterment from the root word "Embitter" is to make a person feel bitter or get aggravated about a certain object, event, and/or person. The essence of a social network hub consisting of pictures, articles, and videos mainly showing those in need aims to bring out the inner human in a person giving insight on how they could help these people in need.
